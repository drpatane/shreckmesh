# What them dice do?

A quick reference for interpreting the dice

# Regular Dice

### ![55yklhj.png\|40](/img/user/9%20Archive/55yklhj.png) Success

Counts as 1 success towards test

--- 
### ![xLRX5kG.png\| 50](/img/user/9%20Archive/xLRX5kG.png) Potential Critical Success

If a roll has a **pair** of these, it is a critical success that counts for **four** regular successes

---
### Blank Face

Regular failure, no extra consequences or effect

--- 
---
# <font style="color:red">Hunger Dice</font>
 
 
 ### ![w1uu19E.png\|50](/img/user/9%20Archive/w1uu19E.png) <font style="color:red">Bestial Failure</font>

If a **failed test** contains one of these, fail task with extra consequences from the Beast

---

### ![VztXJUU.png\|50](/img/user/9%20Archive/VztXJUU.png) <font style="color:red">Messy Critical</font>

If a **critical success** contains at lease one of these, complete task with extra consequences from the Beast (success with consequences)

---
### <font style="color:red">Blank Face</font>

Regular failure, no extra consequences or effect

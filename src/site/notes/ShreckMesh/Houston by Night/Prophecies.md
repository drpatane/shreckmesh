# The Love Letters 

Clan loyalties aside, sometimes the tea is too good to hide.

> [!quote] Fragment 1, "G" to "R," Vellum 1350s 
> "My love. May the words carried upon the back of this peasant body bring you joy and remind you of the vitality of our love. We thrive despite the current apocalypse among the children, although I tire of the incessant scurrying of rats. I cannot wait to drink of you again..."

> [!quote] Fragment 2, "R" to "G," Parchment, 1865
> "Georgios, My scarred warrior . Our mother and I have arrived in this New World at the perfect time. They have murdered the great emancipator, the country cries for revenge. It is wretched silence in comparison to how my form screams for you. The give of your flesh, the warmth of your very essence passing my lips..."

> [!quote] Fragment 3, "G" to "R," Paper, 1988
> "Beloved Rachel. I am called to tend a garden grown over far to the South, to prepare her ground. My every waking night will be sustained by memories of us entwined, dining with those newly transformed. Savored by us though their new virus sees them cast aside. For their vessels of sin are our vessels of love..."

# Deals with Devils

*observed on 5/14/2026*

The Shadow pushed a rolling slate 
Into one of the central rooms in the home of The Statue
Red string and chalk in hand she pointed
At places where the string ended
"Loose ends" 
She called them
First, a newspaper clipping taped to the board 
A white hot dumpster fire burned at the museum 
Presumably holding the body of a rebel 
The rebel's comrade lay immobile in one of The Statue's rooms
What is The Six to do with this captive?

Another string ended with locating the witch Genevieve
Who dealt in magicks that spilled holy blood.

And another led to an address in a wooded land
where The Six could find a hunter of nocturnal game

The Mortician, paler than usual, examines the letters and thinks on this name of Rachel
A name whispered by followers of the dark mother
And Georgio: currently in power, currently outed
This union with another sect 
A powder keg of scandal

The Rider walks to where the captive rebel lay
She woke them with a hand to the neck
The captive resisted
The Shadow intervened menacingly
The rebel was not cowed
The Poet spoke, soft and sweet
She entered alluringly and sat on the bed
She patted the spot next to her
Offering the chance to speak
"Does the manic pixie dream girl thing work for you?"
"It does" The Poet said

The Poet and the Rebel spoke
These letters were a prize
But prizes can also be bait
A pot of honey to draw in the flies 

There were too many questions
Why didn't the Ivory Tower send someone directly to gather the letters? 
Who tipped off the hunters? 

The Poet told the Rebel of the dumpster fire
Likely the ending of the rebel's comrade
The Shadow handed over a newspaper clipping
An image of the conflagration
White phosphorus fire 

"I've seen these weapons before" said the rebel
"So have we" said the Poet
"This was not our fault"

The two reached a semblance of a truce
The Poet and the Rebel swapped numbers
But, the Rider was not having it 
"Dust this one and be done with it!"
"That would start a war" the Poet said

"I need a guarantee" The rebel said
The Shadow offered a pact, a gift, but one she would not partake of herself
The Mortician stepped in, "I'm not connected with either sect. I'll do it."
The Mortician and the Rebel made a pact
To share information between their groups. 
A mutual guarantee to respond to calls for aid. 
"Are we married now?" The Mortician asked
"Is it your first time?" asked The Rebel

Soon The Six were alone
The Shadow said,
"We should handle Genevieve. And, when we find her, she's mine."
The Statue said, "I have an interest in studying blood sorcery and would like to examine her."
The Shadow mentioned an address given to him by a priest
In a suburb outside the loop

# 14 - Witch Hunt

*observed on 6/11/2026*

The Rider, injured and hungry, is fed by the Statue, who also feeds. 
Two lives drained and
Hunger sated for both
At least for now. 

The Shadow, having returned,
Hears the story of the exploding trap 
And tells of a meeting with a keeper
From the Ivory Tower 
Of a gathering of endless night 

The Five departs, looking for the witch
Genevieve 
The Poet and the Shadow
Talk of pasts best left secret
And about how things can change so quickly

The Five arrive. 
A place unseen and hidden
The Poet is filled with fear
And backs away,
But, the Shadow carries her inside

An abandoned house
Roof missing in parts 
House wrap on ceiling

A shuffling 
From up the stairs
A bathrobe and singular croc
The witch herself 

The Shadow asks
"What the fuck happened?"
"Are you here to gloat?"
Asked the Witch
"I wanted to. Thought I outplayed you"
Said the Shadow 
"I think we're both being played"
The Witch replied. 

The Poet thought she recognized the Witch, but sometimes the mind plays tricks. 
"You were there" she said
"Where?" The Witch replied
"I must be mistaking you for someone else"
Said the Poet 

The Shadow began a set of questions 
And the witch confirmed 
The involvement of Frank 
A suit making a play for a power shift
A show of force to keep the cattle in line

"We need to strike the fear of God into them" were the words of Frank that the witch relayed
And, she said one named Heinrich,
who talked about a coven of witches who would do what he wants. 

The witch had given them a warning not to use magic from those followers of the dark mother, but she wasn't listened to. 

Instead, she sprayed holy blood 
Above the altar of a church

The Shadow walks to the witch. 
She resists and the Shadow 
Destroys her shoulder
A cry of pain 
The witch collapses to the ground
In grief and shame 
The Shadow calmly pins her frozen,
Picks her up
And asks for help with the door

The Poet holds it open
The Mortician searches upstairs
The Statue searches downstairs
The Rider leaves on her bike

The Poet watches the Shadow put the Witch in her trunk, 
And sob in the front seat
A few, anguished minutes. 
Then change her clothes and return 

The Statue negotiates to try to host
The witch in their home
The Shadow declines

The Mortician observes
That maybe Frank's plan was revenge for the leader of the magicians being burned
But the Shadow dismisses it as idiocy. 
And, the Mortician also figures that the magic used to conceal this place must have involved the mad ones. 

The Statue hopes that the keeper of the gathering of endless night is not one of them. 

Elsewhere, a Rider stares at an empty motel, while the Shadow, just having deposited a frozen witch, lays on a cot in a sparse room.

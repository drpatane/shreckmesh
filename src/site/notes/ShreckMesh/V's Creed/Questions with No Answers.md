# Questions With No Answers

*observed on 4/2/2026*

Three fruit
Golden in hue 
The Rider was silent
Her face opaque
"What does it mean?"
The Poet asked
"Do these inspire dread?"
The Doctor asked
"I don't know you enough"
The Rider said
"Tell us what we need to take care of"
The Shadow said.
The Rider walked
The Rider rode away.

The Mortician and the Seer searched
And found nothing
The room held nothing 
Only the sacred texts
Of mortals who play acted
A connection with eternity
An empty room with empty pages

"I have a bad feeling"
Said the poet

The Shadow's servant also found nothing
No records of who stayed here
Since the start of the millennium

A message from the Rider: "Run"

The Six left with a plan:
Meet at the Shadow's place
And, the following night, they did

The Seer reminds the Six of her vision
A museum of art
An exhibit of letters
An unsettled feeling
"We need to get to the letters and read them"

The Shadow looks at the Rider
"Are we in danger?"
The Rider responds
"It is someone from my past"
"Do they have a reason to harm you?"
Asked the Mortician
"That is a hard question to answer"
Said the Rider
"What was the message in the pomegranates?"
Asked the Poet
"I am either the Prince or the consort or someone else, but it is not good"
The Rider said.

The Mortician tells us of a piece of papyrus
"New blood with old tricks"
The paper was old, the blood had been painted over many times

The Six plan their next steps
The return of a sleeping man
A visit to an old friend of the Shadow
And a masquerade at a museum
